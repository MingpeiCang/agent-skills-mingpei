# Exemplars

Use these examples to imitate rhetorical movement and sentence control. Do not copy their topic nouns.

## Contribution

Source:
`These results provide numerical evidence for our expectations: reweighting not only produces a correct continuum limit, it also eliminates the large cut-off effects of staggered fermions.`

Rewrite:
`These results provide numerical evidence that reweighting not only yields the correct continuum limit but also eliminates the large cut-off effects of staggered fermions.`

Why it works:
- converts the colon structure into a cleaner `evidence that` frame
- preserves both technical claims

Source:
`The good agreement validates the models and establishes their utility as design tools for optimizing the layouts of the chips and the shapes of the interconnects.`

Rewrite:
`The good agreement validates the models and establishes their utility as design tools for optimizing chip layouts and interconnect geometries.`

Why it works:
- keeps the contribution frame
- compresses the tail without changing content

## Method

Source:
`We employed top-down nanofabrication to create model systems containing pairs of particles at precisely defined distances on aluminium oxide (alumina) and titanium oxide (titania), a nonreducible and a reducible support respectively.`

Rewrite:
`We employ top-down nanofabrication to create model systems containing pairs of particles at precisely defined distances on aluminium oxide (alumina) and titanium oxide (titania), which serve respectively as a nonreducible and a reducible support.`

Why it works:
- clarifies the appositive support description
- keeps the full method chain

Source:
`We determined the population from a direct measurement in the qubit basis and the coherence from a parity oscillation measurement.`

Rewrite:
`We determine the population from direct measurements in the qubit basis and the coherence from parity-oscillation measurements.`

Why it works:
- preserves the paired measurement structure
- improves parallelism

## Result

Source:
`Actuation to higher temperatures was poorly reversible, likely because of an irreversible phase transition.`

Rewrite:
`We find that actuation to higher temperatures is poorly reversible, likely because of an irreversible phase transition.`

Why it works:
- adds a clear result frame
- preserves the causal hedge

Source:
`The numerical complexity associated with time-optimal solutions increases for shorter process durations.`

Rewrite:
`We find that the numerical complexity associated with time-optimal solutions increases as the process duration decreases.`

Why it works:
- makes the monotonic relation explicit
- keeps the exact claim

## Scope Or Implication

Source:
`Neutron scattering is not sensitive to small impurity phases that may influence other bulk characterization techniques.`

Rewrite:
`These findings suggest that neutron scattering is not sensitive to small impurity phases that may influence other bulk characterization techniques.`

Why it works:
- makes the implication frame explicit
- preserves `may influence`

Source:
`On a millimetre to centimetre scale, facets may have already coarsened substantially by the elimination of facets along the front.`

Rewrite:
`This suggests that, on the millimetre-to-centimetre scale, facets may already have coarsened substantially through the elimination of facets along the front.`

Why it works:
- makes the inference explicit
- preserves both scale and hedge

## Transition

Source:
`However, the crystallinity requirement limits the applicability of this approach, and the small number of images make this method sensitive to experimental errors and noise.`

Rewrite:
`However, the crystallinity requirement limits the applicability of this approach, and the small number of images makes the method sensitive to experimental errors and noise.`

Why it works:
- preserves the limitation structure
- fixes agreement without over-rewriting

Source:
`Furthermore, an additional advantage of OPDs is the ease of creating photodetectors with complex shapes.`

Rewrite:
`Furthermore, an additional advantage of OPDs is the ease with which photodetectors with complex shapes can be created.`

Why it works:
- keeps the additive transition
- improves syntactic flow
